Software Highlights:
	GO3Treks allows you to: 
	*Connect to your Personal Ozone Monitor (POM)
	*Download a logged "Trek"
	*Combine ozone data with microAeth black carbon data
	*Automatically create a .kml file to be viewed in Google Earth
	*Allow you to enter a "blog" to summarize your "Trek"
	*Upload the .kml file and "Trek" to www.GO3project.com
